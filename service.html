Clubs, miscellaneous events, etc...

LEO Club

A youth organization affiliated with Lions Clubs International, designed to empower young people to develop leadership skills, gain experience, and have opportunities to serve their communities. The acronym "LEO" stands for Leadership, Experience, and Opportunity.

NTHS

An international honor society for outstanding career and technical students of workforce vocational education institutions.

SkillsUSA

SkillsUSA is a co-curricular program where teachers focus on both subject-specific knowledge and personal development to prepare students for the workforce. SkillsUSA Hawaii offers 17 contests for members in good standing with National SkillsUSA. Membership is available to public school students enrolled in a CTE course related to the events.

INTERACT Club

An Interact Club is a service club for youth, sponsored by a Rotary Club, that focuses on developing leadership skills, promoting international understanding, and engaging in service projects
